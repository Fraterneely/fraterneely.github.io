About KNOWN Corp

Pioneering the future through innovation and technology

Our Story

KNOWN Corp was founded in 2022 by visionaries HOZANA INEZA Fraterne Ely and BAHATI Blaise with a simple yet powerful mission: to harness the potential of technology to solve complex global challenges.

What began as a small startup with big dreams has evolved into a forward-thinking technology company working on innovative solutions across multiple domains. Our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Today, KNOWN Corp stands at the forefront of technological innovation, developing cutting-edge solutions that have the potential to transform industries and improve lives around the world.

Our Mission

To develop innovative technology solutions that address real-world challenges and create meaningful impact. We strive to build products and services that empower individuals, organizations, and communities to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Through our commitment to excellence, integrity, and forward-thinking, we aim to be catalysts for positive change, driving progress and creating value for all our stakeholders.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology serves as a bridge to human potential, where innovation is accessible to all, and where the digital revolution empowers rather than divides.

KNOWN Corp aspires to be a global leader in technology innovation, recognized for our ability to anticipate future needs and develop solutions that make a lasting positive impact on society, the economy, and the environment.

Our Founders

The visionaries behind KNOWN Corp

HOZANA INEZA Fraterne Ely

Founder & CEO

BAHATI Blaise
